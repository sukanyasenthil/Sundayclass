Prof. SVM Satyanarayana

The Selfless Teacher Ever Lived ! The greatest gift to physics students.

Sunday class is unique that it has no formal registration procedures to attend and no fees. It also had a diverse group of learners and that too a fluctuating group. Many who attended his classes were Students & Teachers who were mostly from first generation Learners, different socio-economic background, from other districts,villages,states who travelled overnight to attend his classes on Sundays, some shifted to Chennai to attend them & some travelled across state to Kerala to attend his lectures in APT on the other days.
