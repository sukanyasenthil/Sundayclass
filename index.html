Prof. SVM Satyanarayana

The Selfless Teacher Ever Lived ! The greatest gift to physics students.

In 1996, when SVM Satyanarayana was doing Ph.D at Indira Gandhi Centre of Atomic Research (IGCAR), Kalpakkam he taught in the Summer Training Program in Physics (STIP) organized by IGCAR. A few Students who attended that STIP program and impressed by his teaching, requested him to teach Problem solving in Physics on Sundays during Sundays. He accepted their request and started to teach on Saturdays and Sundays in the Nuclear Physics Department at University of Madras.Â  Sunday class is unique that it has no formal registration procedures to attend and no fees. It also had a diverse group of learners and that too a fluctuating group. Many who attended his classes were Students & Teachers who were mostly from first generation Learners, different socio-economic background, from other districts,villages,states who travelled overnight to attend his classes on Sundays, some shifted to Chennai to attend them & some travelled across state to Kerala to attend his lectures in APT on the other days. Sunday class always ensured that the learners had Psychological safety but Intellectual discomfort. Students are allowed to learn at their own pace and ask questions when they are comfortable. The Sunday class & Satya Sir are synonymous in the lives of numerous Students and Teachers. He always gave his best for them and had a lasting positive impact on them by transforming their lives.
