About Prof. SVM Satyanarayana

The Selfless Teacher Ever Lived ! The greatest gift to physics students.

In 1996, when SVM Satyanarayana was doing Ph.D at Indira Gandhi Centre of Atomic Research (IGCAR), Kalpakkam he taught in the Summer Training Program in Physics (STIP) organized by IGCAR. A few Students who attended that STIP program and impressed by his teaching, requested him to teach Problem solving in Physics on Sundays during Sundays. He accepted their request and started to teach on Saturdays and Sundays in the Nuclear Physics Department at University of Madras.
